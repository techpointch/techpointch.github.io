Features

Zarzul can do a lot of things, here's what's special about it.

Updated Regularly

Zarzul is updated regularly and new features, bug fixes and more are released.

Secure

Zarzul listens to moderation commands like bans or kicks only if the person who executed the command has administrative permissions.

Fun minigames

Zarzul has some fun minigames that anyone can enjoy!

Music

Listen to your favourite songs by your favourite artists right from a Discord Voice Channel!

Memes

Zarzul has a lot of memes that you can laugh at! You can even submit your own on the subreddit r/techpointch

Interactive

Zarzul responds to messages containing "Hi" or "Hello" and greets the users
